Drop thunder recordings here to override synthesized thunder.

Supported filenames (first found are loaded):
- thunder-1.mp3 / thunder-1.ogg / thunder-1.wav
- thunder-2.mp3 / thunder-2.ogg / thunder-2.wav
- thunder-3.mp3 / thunder-3.ogg / thunder-3.wav
- thunder-4.mp3 / thunder-4.ogg / thunder-4.wav

Notes:
- Use 44.1kHz or 48kHz files for best compatibility.
- Mono or stereo both work.
- Keep peaks controlled (-3 dBFS-ish) to avoid clipping in dramatic mixes.
